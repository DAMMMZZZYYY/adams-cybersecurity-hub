# Internal Security Audit - Botium Toys

## Overview
This audit evaluates the current security posture of Botium Toys using the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

## Findings
- High risk score (8/10)
- Lack of encryption
- No access control policies
- No disaster recovery or backups
- Missing IDS

## Controls Assessment
### Administrative Controls
- Weak password policy
- No least privilege

### Technical Controls
- Firewall and antivirus implemented
- No encryption, IDS, or backups

### Physical Controls
- Locks, CCTV, fire systems present

## Compliance
- Not compliant with PCI DSS
- Partially compliant with GDPR

## Conclusion
Botium Toys must implement stronger controls to reduce risks and ensure compliance.
